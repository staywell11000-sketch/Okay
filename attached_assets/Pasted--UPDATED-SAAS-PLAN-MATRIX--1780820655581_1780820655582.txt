==================================================
FEATURE GATING
==================================================

Implement centralized feature gating.

Organizations should only access features included in their plan.

Examples:

Starter:
- Cannot access Team page
- Cannot access AI Intelligence
- Cannot access Automations

Professional:
- Can access Team
- Cannot access AI Intelligence
- Cannot access Advanced Automations

Agency:
- Full access

Unauthorized feature access should display:

"This feature is not included in your current plan. Upgrade your subscription to continue."
